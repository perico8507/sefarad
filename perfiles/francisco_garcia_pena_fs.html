Transcripción Paleográfica

Francisco Garcia Peña. 1883–1962. Birth: 4 October 1883, General Cepeda, Coahuila, Mexico. Death: 9 May 1962, General Cepeda, Coahuila, Mexico. Spouse: Martina Garcia Rodriguez 1887–1962. Parents: Mateo Garcia Flores 1851–1904, Lucia de la Peña Saucedo 1850–1930.
